# DJ MANIKANTA Portfolio - Contact Form Guide

## How Contact Form Works

### For Website Visitors (Your Clients):

1. **They fill the form on your website:**
   - Name
   - Email
   - Project Type (e.g., "Video Editing for YouTube")
   - Message (details about their project)

2. **They click "Send Message"**
   - See success notification: "Message Sent!"
   - Form clears automatically

### For You (DJ Manikanta):

## How to Check New Client Inquiries

### Step 1: Open Admin Dashboard
Go to this URL: **https://manikanta-motion.preview.emergentagent.com/admin**

**BOOKMARK THIS PAGE** - This is where all your client messages appear!

### Step 2: View All Submissions
You'll see:
- **Stats at top:**
  - Total Submissions
  - New (Unread) messages
  - This Month's count

- **List of all messages:**
  Each card shows:
  - Client's name
  - Email address
  - Date & time they submitted
  - Project type
  - Full message content
  - "New" badge for unread messages

### Step 3: Reply to Clients
- Click **"Reply via Email"** button
- Your email app opens automatically
- Client's email is already filled in
- Subject line is pre-written
- Type your response and send!
